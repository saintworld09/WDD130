Lagos state

Lagos State, often referred to simply as Lagos, is not just Nigeria's largest city; it's also one of the fastest-growing megacities in the world. Situated in the southwestern part of Nigeria, Lagos is a bustling metropolis where various ethnic groups from across Nigeria and beyond converge, creating a colorful mosaic of cultures, traditions, and languages.
